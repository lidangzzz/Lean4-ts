# AI on Mobile Devices: Qualcomm's Perspective

Qualcomm has pioneered the transformation of mobile devices into powerful AI inference engines through our industry-leading Snapdragon platforms. At the heart of this revolution is the Qualcomm Hexagon Processor, our dedicated AI acceleration hardware integrated into Snapdragon chipsets. The latest Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 and Snapdragon X Elite platforms feature our most advanced AI engines yet, capable of delivering up to 75 TOPS (Trillion Operations Per Second) of AI performance. This massive computational power, combined with our specialized Qualcomm AI Engine, enables smartphones and laptops to run sophisticated machine learning models entirely on-device, from natural language processing to computer vision applications, without relying on cloud connectivity.

On-device AI represents a fundamental shift in how we approach artificial intelligence, prioritizing privacy, latency, and reliability. Qualcomm's AI Stack provides developers with a comprehensive suite of tools to optimize and deploy AI models directly on Snapdragon-powered devices. This approach eliminates the need to send sensitive user data to remote servers, addressing growing privacy concerns while delivering sub-millisecond response times for critical applications. Our Snapdragon Sense technology demonstrates this capability in action, powering features like real-time language translation, voice-activated assistants, and intelligent camera enhancements that process images in nanoseconds, all while consuming minimal power through our heterogeneous computing architecture.

The future of mobile AI extends far beyond smartphones into the broader intelligent edge ecosystem. Qualcomm's Snapdragon Ride platform brings autonomous driving capabilities to vehicles, while our AI-enabled IoT solutions power smart cities, industrial automation, and healthcare monitoring systems. Looking ahead, we are investing heavily in generative AI capabilities that can run entirely on-device, with our latest platforms supporting large language models with up to 10 billion parameters directly on mobile hardware. This commitment to on-device AI processing, combined with our advances in 5G connectivity and edge computing, positions Qualcomm at the forefront of democratizing AI technology, making it accessible, private, and ubiquitous across all aspects of daily life.
