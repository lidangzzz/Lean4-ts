# National AI Strategy and Economic Diversification: United Arab Emirates

## The UAE AI Strategy 2031

The United Arab Emirates has positioned itself as a global pioneer in artificial intelligence governance and innovation. In October 2017, the UAE became the first country in the world to establish a dedicated Ministry of State for Artificial Intelligence, appointing Omar bin Sultan Al Olama as the first Minister of State for Artificial Intelligence, Digital Economy and Remote Work Applications. This historic move signaled the nation's commitment to becoming a world leader in AI by 2031. The UAE Strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2031 was launched with the ambitious vision to transform the country into a global AI leader by investing in people and industries that are key to success. The strategy is built on eight strategic pillars: education, government services, healthcare, transportation, renewable energy, technology, space exploration, and security. These pillars guide the integration of AI across vital sectors of the economy and society, positioning the UAE as a testbed for AI innovation and a destination for AI investment.

## AI Ministry and Governance Infrastructure

The UAE has developed a comprehensive institutional framework to support its AI ambitions. The AI and Blockchain Council was established to propose policies that create an AI-friendly ecosystem, while the UAE Digital Economy Council works to create a thriving digital economy that combines human ingenuity with advanced technologies. Key initiatives include the "1 Million AI Talents" program, which aims to train 1 million people in AI by 2027, positioning the UAE as a global leader in AI advancement. The Coders HQ initiative, launched in partnership with over 40 companies, redefines local coding communities and empowers the next wave of developers. The UAE AI Camp represents the biggest gathering of trainings, workshops, webinars, and talks about artificial intelligence in the region. The UAE Charter for the Development and Use of Artificial Intelligence provides ethical guidelines for AI development and deployment, ensuring responsible innovation.

## Economic Diversification Through AI

AI serves as a cornerstone of the UAE's broader economic diversification strategy, which aims to reduce dependence on oil revenues and transition toward a knowledge-based economy. The government has recognized that AI adoption can boost GDP significantly while improving government efficiency by 100% through AI integration in public services. The strategy focuses on creating a new economic sector centered on AI, attracting international talent and investment, and developing AI-related education and research capabilities. Initiatives like the UAE Artificial Intelligence Award honor excellence in applying AI-powered solutions across federal, local, semi-governmental, academic, and private sector entities. The Digital Economy agenda aims to make the UAE "the most prosperous digital economy among digitally developed countries." Through strategic partnerships with global technology leaders, investment in AI infrastructure, and development of local talent, the UAE is working to establish itself as a regional hub for AI innovation and a model for other nations seeking to harness AI for economic transformation.
